    • how to make a to-do list

    • Decide what medium works best for you.
    • List all of the tasks that you need to accomplish.
    • Outsource any tasks you can.
    • Sort your master list into categories.
    • Make your to-do lists for the current day only.
    • Estimate the time each task will take.
    • Write down due dates next to every task on your master list.
    • Share your list with someone.
